About Us

Newton Youth Sports was built by Newton parents who got tired of Googling 15 different websites to find a basketball league for their kid.

The Problem We're Solving

Newton has an incredible youth sports ecosystem — over 20 organizations running 166 programs across 14 sports. But finding them? That's another story. Information is scattered across dozens of websites, Facebook groups, word-of-mouth recommendations, and flyers taped to the wall at Cabot's.

We built Newton Youth Sports to put everything in one place. Every program, every season, every age group. Search, filter, compare, and plan — all without creating an account or downloading an app.
